# DLSS 5 vs DLSS 4 vs FSR 4: The 2026 Upscaling War, Explained

*7 min read · Last updated April 2026*

---

In 2026, the GPU you buy isn't just a question of teraflops. It's a question of which AI reconstruction ecosystem you're committing to — and what that means for how your games look and perform for the next three to four years.

Here's the honest breakdown.

---

## The Big Picture in One Sentence Each

**DLSS 5** — NVIDIA's bet that AI can *replace* lighting calculations, not just assist them. Exclusive to RTX 50 series. Launches Fall 2026. Controversial.

**DLSS 4 / 4.5** — Frame generation that multiplies your fps by up to 6x. Best upscaling tech available today. Requires RTX 40 or 50 series for frame gen; upscaling works on all RTX cards.

**FSR 4** — AMD finally went fully AI-powered. Now genuinely competitive with DLSS 4 in image quality. Works on RDNA 4 GPUs only for AI features; older GPUs get FSR 3.1 (algorithmic, not AI).

---

## What Each Technology Actually Does

### DLSS 4 / 4.5 (Available Now)

DLSS 4 uses a transformer-based neural network to upscale lower-resolution frames to your target resolution. DLSS 4.5 adds a second-generation transformer model with better temporal stability and fewer ghosting artifacts.

The killer feature is Multi Frame Generation:
- **DLSS 4**: Generate up to 4 frames for every 1 rendered frame → up to 4× fps multiplier
- **DLSS 4.5 Dynamic MFG**: Up to 6× multiplier, automatically adjusting based on your monitor's refresh rate

In practice, most major releases in 2026 support both DLSS and FSR, so your choice of GPU brand doesn't lock you out of upscaling. The difference is quality: DLSS 4 > FSR 4 > FSR 3.1 > XeSS, with the gap between DLSS 4 and FSR 4 being smaller than previous generations.

### DLSS 5 (Fall 2026)

DLSS 5 doesn't improve frame rates. It improves what each frame *looks like* by replacing the game's lighting model with an AI-synthesized version. Think of it as a post-processing layer that understands physics.

It runs *on top of* DLSS 4.5 — so on an RTX 5090, you get 6× frame generation AND neural rendering simultaneously.

**RTX 50 series only.** No exceptions confirmed yet.

### FSR 4 (Available Now, RDNA 4 only)

With FSR 4, AMD has moved to machine learning–based upscaling and frame generation, and those features only run on the latest RDNA 4 GPUs. That means if you're not on new AMD hardware, you don't get the full package.

The quality gap between FSR 4 and DLSS 4 is now smaller than it has ever been. For RX 9070 XT and RX 9070 owners, FSR 4 is an excellent choice. For everyone on older AMD hardware, FSR 3.1 is still available and works everywhere.

## Should You Upgrade for DLSS 5?

This is the real question, and the honest answer is: **probably not yet, and maybe not because of DLSS 5.**

**If you have an RTX 40 series:** You already have the best performance upscaling available. DLSS 4.5 is excellent. Wait and see how DLSS 5 looks at launch — its current demos are six months old and were running on pre-release hardware.

**If you have an RTX 30 series or older:** DLSS 5 is one reason to upgrade, but DLSS 4's Multi Frame Generation (which you're currently locked out of) is a more immediate improvement to your daily gaming experience.

**If you're buying new:** RTX 50 series gives you the full stack — DLSS 4.5 Multi Frame Gen now, DLSS 5 in the fall. The RTX 5070 Ti is the price/performance sweet spot at launch.

**If you're on AMD:** FSR 4 is genuinely excellent. Unless you specifically want DLSS 5 Neural Rendering, there's no need to switch ecosystems.

# DLSS 5 vs DLSS 4 vs FSR 4：2026 超分辨率大战完全解析

*阅读约7分钟 · 2026年4月*

---

在 2026 年，买什么显卡已经不只是 TFLOPS 的问题，而是你要押注哪个 AI 重建生态——以及这个选择在未来三四年里如何影响你的游戏画面和性能。

以下是不废话的完整对比。

---

## 一句话总结

**DLSS 5** — NVIDIA 的赌注：让 AI *替代* 光照计算，而不只是辅助它。仅支持 RTX 50 系列，2026 年秋季发布。存在争议。

**DLSS 4 / 4.5** — 帧生成技术，将你的帧率最高乘以 6 倍。当下最佳超分技术。帧生成需要 RTX 40 或 50 系列；超分辨率支持所有 RTX 显卡。

**FSR 4** — AMD 终于全面转向 AI 驱动。图像质量现在与 DLSS 4 真正接近。AI 功能仅限 RDNA 4 GPU；老卡仍可使用 FSR 3.1（算法版，非 AI）。

---

## 各技术实际做了什么

### DLSS 4 / 4.5（现已可用）

DLSS 4 使用 Transformer 架构神经网络，将低分辨率帧放大到目标分辨率。DLSS 4.5 加入第二代 Transformer 模型，进一步提升时序稳定性、减少鬼影。

杀手功能是多帧生成：DLSS 4 每渲染 1 帧最多生成 4 帧（4× FPS 乘数）；DLSS 4.5 动态 MFG 最高 6× 乘数，根据你的显示器刷新率自动调整。

### DLSS 5（2026 年秋季）

DLSS 5 不提升帧率。它用 AI 合成版本替换游戏的光照模型，改变每一帧*看起来什么样*。把它想象成一个理解物理的后处理层。

它运行在 DLSS 4.5 之上——所以 RTX 5090 上你可以同时拥有 6× 帧生成 AND 神经渲染。**仅限 RTX 50 系列。**

### FSR 4（现已可用，仅限 RDNA 4）

AMD 终于全面转向机器学习超分和帧生成。这些功能仅在 RDNA 4 GPU 上运行。FSR 4 与 DLSS 4 之间的质量差距现在是历代最小的。对于老 AMD 显卡，FSR 3.1 仍然全平台可用。

## 应该为了 DLSS 5 升级显卡吗？

直接答案：**现在可能不值得，而且也许并非因为 DLSS 5。**

**RTX 40 系列用户：** 你已经有了当下最好的性能超分。等等看 DLSS 5 正式发布后的实际表现——当前演示已经是六个月前的预发布硬件结果。

**RTX 30 系列或更老：** DLSS 5 是升级的理由之一，但 DLSS 4 的多帧生成（你目前无法使用）是对日常游戏体验更直接的改善。

**准备新购：** RTX 50 系列给你完整的技术栈——现在有 DLSS 4.5 多帧生成，秋天有 DLSS 5。RTX 5070 Ti 是性价比最优的选择。

**AMD 用户：** FSR 4 真的很优秀。除非你特别想要 DLSS 5 神经渲染，否则没必要换生态。
